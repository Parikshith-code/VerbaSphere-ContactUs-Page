‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive a response?

+

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours. Live chat typically has response times of 2-5 minutes, while email responses may take up to one business day depending on the complexity of your question.

Do you offer technical support on weekends?

+

Yes! Our live chat support is available 24/7, including weekends and holidays. Email support operates with reduced staff on weekends but urgent matters are still addressed.

How can I report a bug or suggest a feature?

+

You can report bugs or suggest features through our contact form by selecting "Bug Report" or "Feature Request" in the subject dropdown. Our development team reviews these submissions regularly.

Are there support options for enterprise clients?

+

Absolutely! Enterprise clients receive dedicated account managers and priority support channels. Please contact our business team at business@verbasphere.com to learn more about our enterprise support packages.
